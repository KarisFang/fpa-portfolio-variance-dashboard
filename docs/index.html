The FY2025 plan assumed a uniform +10% revenue lift across every region. Greater Asia missed it by −43.5% while North America beat it by +12.6% — one region's miss erased most of the company's outperformance elsewhere. On the cost side, General & Administrative overspend never exceeded ~2% in any single month, but compounded to +11.7% by year-end — invisible to a monthly-only check, obvious in a YTD view.
