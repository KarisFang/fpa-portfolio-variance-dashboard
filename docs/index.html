▸ FLAGGED

The FY2025 plan assumed a uniform +10% revenue lift across every region. Greater Asia missed that plan by −43.5% while North America beat it by +12.6%. One region's miss erased almost all of the company's outperformance elsewhere — a flat company-wide variance number would have hidden this completely.

▸ FLAGGED

General & Administrative overspend never exceeded ~2% in any single month, but crept up every month, compounding to +11.7% by year-end. Invisible to a monthly threshold check — obvious in a YTD view. This is the slow-creep variance pattern a monthly (not just annual) review exists to catch.
